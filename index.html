What We Do

2KHA.com is the first product of Bambou Group, also the first in Myanmar, an open directory service which enables both local and international users to search local businesses and interesting places either in Burmese or in English language. We enlist business information across varied sectors like Restaurants, Hotels, Auto Care, Home Decor, Personal and Pet Care, Fitness, Real Estate, Sports, Schools, etc. from all over the country.
